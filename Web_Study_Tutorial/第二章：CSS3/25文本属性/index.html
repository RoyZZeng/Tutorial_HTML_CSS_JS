Web Art, also known as Internet Art, represents a form of digital artwork that exists on or is created using the internet as its primary platform. This genre of art uniquely blends technology and creativity, allowing artists to explore new interactive and dynamic methods of expression that often challenge traditional boundaries of art. With its global reach, Web Art not only democratizes the art world by making it more accessible to a wider audience but also enables innovative collaborations and cultural exchanges, thus reflecting the ever-evolving digital landscape of our time.
